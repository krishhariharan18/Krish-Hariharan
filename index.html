About Me [edit]

I'm Krish Hariharan, a pre-final-year CSE student specializing in IoT at Shiv Nadar University Chennai. I'm someone who's always curious to learn new things, especially languages—both the ones humans speak and the ones computers understand. I enjoy web design and have a keen interest in machine learning and deep learning. Outside of tech, you'll usually find me listening to music or painting. From being a House Captain in school to a Class Representative in college, I've always enjoyed connecting with people and taking on new responsibilities.[1]

Education [edit]

I completed my schooling at Chinmaya Vidyalaya Taylors Road, where I pursued Mathematics with Computer Science in my higher secondary years. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Internet of Things (IoT) at Shiv Nadar University Chennai.[2]
